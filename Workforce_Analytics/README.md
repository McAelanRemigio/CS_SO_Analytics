# Labor Market Analysis for CSU Statewide Workforce Proposal

## Overview

This project documents an independently conducted labor market analysis completed in support of a multi-institution California State University statewide workforce proposal.

The analysis supported the submission of a **$2 million workforce and economic development grant** through California’s Employment Development Department, funded by the U.S. Department of Labor.

## Purpose

The purpose of this work was to provide structured, reproducible labor market intelligence to support grant planning, employer targeting, workforce alignment, and long-term program development.

The analysis focused on identifying relevant manufacturing employers, associated job roles, and supporting data structures needed for proposal development and future workforce planning.

## Scope of Work

Key deliverables included:

- A curated list of **25 manufacturing employers**
- Associated relevant job roles for each employer
- A detailed data dictionary
- Calculations and methodology documentation
- Workflow documentation
- Analytical log tracking assumptions, decisions, and process steps
- Structured outputs designed for reuse beyond the initial grant submission

## Outputs

The project produced organized and reproducible materials to support:

- Grant narrative development
- Employer and industry alignment
- Workforce demand analysis
- Cross-institution collaboration
- Future updates and extensions of the analysis

## Methodology

The analysis was conducted independently and emphasized transparency, consistency, and reproducibility. Documentation was created alongside the analytical work to ensure that future users could understand the data structure, calculations, assumptions, and workflow.

## Impact

The completed analysis was used in the submission of a statewide workforce and economic development grant proposal. The outputs were designed not only to support the immediate grant application, but also to serve as a foundation for continued workforce planning and employer engagement.
